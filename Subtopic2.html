What are the components of a PC?

URL How to Build a PC: The Ultimate Beginner's Guide
Site Author John Burek
Abstract In this article the author John Burek explains step by step how to build youw own PC and what are the things someone needs to consider if they want to embark into this process. He explains how to choose the key components and what their function in the PC system is. As an example, the author tells the process of how he builds his own PC, and what are the exact components that he's getting and what his considerations when buying component parts are. He also discusses whether RGB is really necessary or not, and what are the price considerations someone should have if they want to build their PC. Finally, he explains with heavy detail how to assemble the PC.

URL How to Build a Gaming PC
Site Author Intel Author
Abstract In this article in the Intel website, you can learn how to build a PC from scratch. This article mentions things that were not mentioned in the first article by Burek. The article mentions how it's important to have a clean and large workspace, what type of screwdriver is best to use when building a PC, and what kind of USB flash drive is needed to transfer and install files into your new device. The article goes in depth on the components needed to build your PC, and how to assemble each component in order to keep the whole system protected.
