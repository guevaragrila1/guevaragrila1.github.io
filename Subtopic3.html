Site Author FutureLearn Author
Abstract This article mentions how important PCs have become for our daily lives. We use them for a variety of everyday tasks and routines. Despite of this, the majority of people are not knowledgeable on the systems and processes that take place in order for a PC to access the internet, and be able to be used. The article mentions how learning about computers allows the users to be more conscious about the balance that is required when having on and off screen time. Computers have become an essential part of our daily lives, and more and more people are being born as digital natives. Having knowledge on these machines that are so essential for our daily lives is absolutely necessary for a responsible use of technology.
